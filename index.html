About Me

Hi! I'm Favour, a final-year Microbiology student at MOUAU with a deep curiosity for how things work whether that's microorganisms under a microscope or code running in a browser. I'm currently transitioning into full-stack web development, and I'm loving every step of the journey.

Before diving into tech, I gained hands-on experience in customer service and virtual assistance, which taught me how to communicate clearly, stay organized, and actually listen to what people need. Those skills carry directly into how I think about building websites — with the user always in mind.

I'm learning web development because I want to build real things that solve real problems. My goal is to become a full-stack developer who can take an idea from zero to a fully working product. When I'm not coding, you'll find me reading, working on my fitness, or exploring new ways to grow online.
